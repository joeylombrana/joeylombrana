Lead Capture & Organization System

This is a simple system I built to improve my own lead flow, response time, and organization when working with OPT! and Switchboard Funnel.

What This System Does

  • Captures leads from a clean, professional page
  • Sends an automatic response to new prospects
  • Organizes lead data for easy import into OPT!
  • Helps you respond faster and stay consistent

Generate High-Interest Leads

This system is designed to help you capture and organize high-interest leads — people who are actively looking for information and willingly submitting their contact details.

  • Use your own social media or personal outreach
  • Send prospects directly to your custom page
  • Capture inbound interest instead of chasing cold leads
  • Build your own pipeline without relying on purchased leads

Instead of working through recycled or shared lead lists, this approach allows you to create a more consistent and controlled source of inbound opportunities.

Why I Built It

  • Keep my leads organized
  • Improve response speed
  • Reduce manual work
  • Fit directly into the tools we already use
